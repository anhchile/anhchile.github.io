"Buy less. Choose well. Make it last."

I like the way fashion keeps changing and returning overtime because it brings me more energy and motivates my creativity. However, I prefer the kind of fashion that can last long and never seems to be outdated. That's why after experimenting different styles in my fashion design projects, I choose for myself a style that keeps me staying simple, chic with a few trendy items.

To stay constant with my choosing concept, I start learning and discovering things which are very essence of fashion: silhouettes, pattern cutting, fabric research, and trend research. In this personal blog, I would like to share my own experiences in my fashion career and hope you enjoy it!
